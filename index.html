Krav Maga is a military self-defence and fighting system developed for the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) and Israeli security forces.

It has been created by Imi Lichtenfeld following the understanding that fighting in the street was very different than in a club. In the beginning, it was made for the military force. Then, it has been democratize for the civil self defence.

This is a mix of differents sport like Aikido, Boxing, Wrestling, Judo and Karate. The main concept is to take all the good part from the differents disciplines.

Krav Maga encourages students to avoid physical confrontation. If this is impossible or unsafe, it promotes finishing a fight as quickly and aggressively as possible.

Student learn how to defend against all variety of attacks, including weapon like knife, rope, metal bar, baseball batt, gun, fist, foot, knee, ... and are taught to counter in the quickest and most efficient way. The goal is to reflect the reality, so based on the real-world situation and efficiency.

Krav Maga, so that one may walk in peace.
